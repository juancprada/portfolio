NBA League Pass

Social Media Content, E-Mail Marketing,
Out of Home Advertising
VMLY&R
2018 → 2020

NBA League Pass is a streaming service that allows basketball fans to watch NBA games from their device of choice, live and on-demand.

Alongside a team from VMLY&R New York, Bogotá and Kansas City, I helped build multiple assets (display ads, social media content, e-mails) to promote this service to a global audience during the 2018/19 and 2019/20 seasons, offering free trials and special discounts, while highlighting its features and, of course, the action and excitement of NBA basketball.
